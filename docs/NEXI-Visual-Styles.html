TrueOmni / NEXI Kiosk

Visual Style Exploration

Five distinct design directions for the NEXI hotel self check-in kiosk. Each style is a complete visual system — palette, typography, components, and real mock screens at kiosk resolution (1920x1080 landscape).

Physical touchscreen | 16:9 landscape | 4-star+ hotel lobbies | 1-3 minute sessions

Style 01

Midnight Luxe

Luxury hotel lobby at night. Deep blacks and navies with electric blue accents and warm gold highlights. Glassmorphism cards, ambient glow effects, cinematic photography. Think W Hotels, Edition Hotels, Aman — premium, sophisticated, moody.

References: Apple TV+ interface, luxury hotel branding, dark mode premium apps

Style 02

Nordic Clean

Scandinavian hotel meets digital clarity. Bright, airy, and minimal with exceptional whitespace and typography-driven hierarchy. Clean shadows, pill-shaped buttons, and a calm blue accent. Think Muji, Kinfolk magazine, and Apple Health.

References: Apple Health app, Airbnb checkout, Scandinavian hotel design
