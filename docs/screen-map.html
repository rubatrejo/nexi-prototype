Hardware Reference

HW
Camera
Integrated above the screen. Used for facial recognition match with ID. Also houses microphone.
HW
Touchscreen
Main display. Landscape 1920x1080 or Portrait 1080x1920. Tilted ~15-20 degrees for ergonomics.
HW
TruePay Terminal
Payment terminal mounted on the right side. Chip, NFC/contactless, PIN pad. Verifone/Ingenico style.
HW
ID / Passport Scanner
Horizontal slot below the screen on the front column face. Scans ID cards and passports.
HW
Key Card Dispenser
Below the ID scanner. Dispenses programmed key cards for room access.
HW
Speaker
Rear-mounted. For AI Avatar voice responses, audio feedback, and accessibility.
HW
Glass Shelf
Waist-height shelf extending both sides. For personal belongings during interaction.
HW
Staff Override Button
Physical button on the rear bottom of the kiosk. Triggers admin access.
03

Idle / Screensaver

S
IDL-01 — Screensaver
Full-screen video loop uploaded by hotel via CMS. Cinematic content — hotel exterior, lobby, pool, rooms. Overlaid: "Touch to begin" call-to-action. NEXI logo + "made by TrueOmni" badge. Hotel logo. Time/date. Touch anywhere → Onboarding.
The video is the first impression. It must feel premium. The CTA needs to be visible but not obtrusive — bottom center, subtle pulse animation.
04

Onboarding

S
ONB-01 — Welcome
Hotel branding prominent. "Welcome to [Hotel Name]". Brief intro: "I'm NEXI, your self-service concierge." Skip button available. Auto-advance after 4s.
S
ONB-02 — What You Can Do
Visual overview of available modules: Check-in, Check-out, Room Service, Wayfinding, etc. Icon + label for each. "Let's get started" CTA.
S
ONB-03 — Language Selection (Future)
V2: Language picker grid with flags. V1: Skip, English only.
Onboarding should be skippable. Repeat visitors will want to jump straight to dashboard. Consider: after first use, skip onboarding if guest is recognized.
05

Dashboard

The root of everything. All modules accessible from here. Check-in and Check-out have highest visual hierarchy. AI Avatar chat bubble is persistent here.

S
DSH-01 — Main Dashboard
Header: Hotel logo, time/date, language toggle (future), accessibility icon (wheelchair).
Hero CTAs: CHECK-IN (primary, large) + CHECK-OUT (secondary, large). These dominate the screen.
Module grid: Booking, Room Service, Wayfinding, Events, Listings, Wi-Fi, FAQ, Upsells/Upgrades. Each as card with icon + label.
AI Avatar: Chat bubble floating bottom-right. Tap to open avatar conversation.
Footer: "made by TrueOmni" + NEXI logo.
Ads: Banner ad zone (if hotel configured) — header or inline position.
Module grid is dynamic — hotel enables/disables modules via CMS. If hotel has no spa, no spa card. If no events feed, no events card. The grid adapts.
06

Global UI Elements

O
GBL-01 — Top Navigation Bar
Persistent on all screens (except idle). Hotel logo left, time/date center, back button, home button, accessibility icon, language toggle (future).
O
GBL-02 — Progress Indicator
Step indicator for multi-step flows (check-in, booking, etc.). Shows current step + total steps. E.g. "Step 3 of 7".
O
GBL-03 — Back / Home Buttons
Back arrow (previous screen) + Home icon (back to dashboard) always accessible in nav bar. Confirmation modal if mid-flow: "Are you sure? Your progress will be lost."
M
GBL-04 — Exit Flow Confirmation
Modal: "Are you sure you want to exit? Your progress will not be saved." Buttons: Continue / Exit to Dashboard.
O
GBL-05 — Loading / Processing
Full-screen or inline loading state with animation. Used during: payment processing, ID verification, key card programming, data fetch. Branded animation (NEXI logo pulse or custom).
O
GBL-06 — Success State
Check mark animation + success message. Used after: payment complete, check-in complete, booking confirmed. Auto-advances after 3s or on touch.
O
GBL-07 — Keyboard (On-Screen)
Virtual keyboard for text input. Used for: reservation code entry, name entry, search. Full QWERTY with number row. Large touch targets for kiosk use.
07

Check-in Flow

The star flow. ~3 minutes end to end. 13 screens + branches.

01
CKI-01 — Identification Method
S Two options: "Scan QR from your app" (camera activates) OR "Enter reservation code" (keyboard). Guest picks one.
02a
CKI-02a — QR Scan
S Camera viewfinder active. "Hold your QR code to the camera." Visual guide frame. Auto-detects and advances. HW Camera active.
02b
CKI-02b — Manual Entry
S On-screen keyboard. Two fields: Reservation Code + Last Name. "Find My Reservation" button.
03
CKI-03 — Reservation Summary
S Shows: Guest name, check-in/check-out dates, room type, number of guests, any pre-paid amount. "Confirm & Continue" button.
Branch: Room not ready
03b
CKI-03b — Early Arrival
S "Your room is being prepared." Two options: "Store my luggage & notify me when ready" (free) or "Early check-in for $XX" (if available). See Early Check-in module.
04
CKI-04 — ID Scan Instruction
S Animation showing where to place ID/passport on the scanner below. "Place your ID face-down on the scanner below." Arrow pointing down to physical scanner. HW Scanner activates.
05
CKI-05 — ID Scanning
O Processing animation. "Reading your document..." Progress indicator.
Branch: Scan error
05e
CKI-05e — ID Scan Error
E "We couldn't read your document. Please try again." Instructions to flip/reposition. Attempt counter (1/3, 2/3). After 3rd fail: "Request Assistance" button appears. HW Scanner retries.
06
CKI-06 — Facial Recognition
S Camera feed showing guest's face with alignment guide (oval frame). "Look at the camera above." Auto-captures when face is centered. HW Camera captures.
07
CKI-07 — Identity Verified
O Success state: "Identity verified" with check animation. Shows ID photo vs. camera photo side by side briefly. Auto-advances.
08
CKI-08 — Terms & Agreement
S Hotel policies summary (scrollable). Digital signature area (finger draw on touch screen). "I agree & continue" button.
09
CKI-09 — Upsell Opportunity
S "Upgrade your stay?" Room upgrade options with photos, price difference. Late check-out option. "No thanks" + "Upgrade" buttons. See Upsells module.
10
CKI-10 — Payment
S If balance due: Itemized charges summary. Total amount. "Pay now" CTA pointing to TruePay terminal on the right. "Tap, insert, or swipe your card on the terminal." HW TruePay activates. See TruePay module.
11
CKI-11 — Additional Guests
S "Are there additional guests?" Yes/No. If yes: additional guest scans ID only (no facial). Each gets their own key card. Titular authorizes. Loop for each guest.
12
CKI-12 — Key Card Dispensing
S Animation showing key card being dispensed below. "Your key card is ready! Please take it from the slot below." Room number displayed prominently. HW Dispenser activates. Repeat per guest.
13
CKI-13 — Confirmation & Welcome
S "You're all set!" Room number large. Floor/wing info. Mini map to room (or "Where is my room?" CTA → Wayfinding). Receipt options: Email / Phone / QR. "Enjoy your stay" + "Back to Home" buttons. Wi-Fi credentials shown.
08

Check-out Flow

01
CKO-01 — Identification
S Same as check-in: QR scan or reservation code + last name.
02
CKO-02 — Stay Summary & Charges
S Itemized list: room charges, room service, minibar, late fees, etc. Total balance. "Pay & Check Out" CTA.
03
CKO-03 — Payment
S TruePay payment flow (if balance > 0). Same pattern as check-in payment. HW TruePay.
04
CKO-04 — Return Key
S "Please insert your key card in the slot below." Animation pointing to dispenser slot. HW Key slot accepts return. Or: "Keep your key card as a souvenir" (if hotel allows).
05
CKO-05 — Satisfaction Survey
S Quick 3-question survey. 1) Overall rating (1-5 stars or emoji scale). 2) "What did you enjoy most?" (multiple choice). 3) "Anything we can improve?" (optional text). "Submit" or "Skip" buttons.
06
CKO-06 — Farewell & Upsell
S "Thank you for staying with us!" + "Book your next stay and save 15%" CTA (optional, hotel configured). Receipt options: Email / Phone / QR. "Done" button → idle.
09

Booking (Walk-in)

For guests without a reservation who walk in and want to book + check in on the spot.

01
BKG-01 — Date Selection
S Calendar picker. Check-in date + Check-out date. Number of nights auto-calculated. "Next" button.
02
BKG-02 — Guest Count
S Adults + Children counters (+ / - buttons). "Search Availability" CTA.
03
BKG-03 — Room Selection
S Available rooms as cards: room photo, type name, amenities icons, price per night, total. Filter/sort options. "Select" button per room.
04
BKG-04 — Room Detail
S Photo gallery of selected room. Full amenity list. Bed configuration. Floor/view info. "Book This Room" CTA.
05
BKG-05 — Upsells
S "Enhance your stay." Breakfast package, parking, late checkout, room upgrade. Each with price. Add/skip.
06
BKG-06 — Booking Summary
S Full summary: dates, room, guests, add-ons, total price. "Confirm & Pay" CTA.
07
BKG-07 — Payment
S TruePay flow. HW Terminal activates.
08
BKG-08 — Booking Confirmed
S "Booking confirmed!" Reservation number displayed. "Proceed to Check-in" CTA → starts check-in flow. Or "Done" → dashboard.

Room Service

01
RSV-01 — Guest Identification
S QR scan or reservation code to identify guest and room number.
02
RSV-02 — Menu Categories
S Category grid: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks, Beverages, Desserts. Photos per category. Managed via CMS by hotel.
03
RSV-03 — Menu Items
S Item list within category: photo, name, description, price, dietary tags (vegan, GF, etc.). "Add to order" button. Quantity selector.
04
RSV-04 — Item Detail
S Full photo, detailed description, ingredients, allergen info, customization options (e.g. "No onions"). "Add to Order" CTA.
05
RSV-05 — Cart / Order Review
S All items, quantities, prices. Edit/remove. Subtotal + tax + service fee. Special instructions text field.
06
RSV-06 — Delivery Schedule
S "When would you like your order?" Two options: "As soon as possible" (with estimated time) OR "Schedule" (date/time picker within stay dates).
07
RSV-07 — Payment Method
S "Pay now" (TruePay) OR "Charge to room [XXX]". HW TruePay if paying now.
08
RSV-08 — Order Confirmation
S "Order placed!" Order number, estimated delivery time, room number. Receipt options. "Back to Dashboard" CTA.
11

Wayfinding

01
WAY-01 — Destination Selection
S Grid of POIs: My Room (requires ID), Pool, Restaurant, Gym, Spa, Meeting Rooms, Lobby, Parking, etc. Search bar. Each with icon + name. Hotel configures via CMS.
02
WAY-02 — Interactive Route Map
S Floor plan map. Blue dot = kiosk location. Animated path from kiosk to destination. Floor transitions if multi-story (elevator/stairs indicator). Estimated walk time. Zoom/pan touchable. "Get new directions" button.
The "My Room" option requires guest to identify first (QR or code) to know their room number. Other destinations are open to anyone.

EVT-01 — Events Feed

EVT-02 — Event Detail

Upsells & Upgrades

Appears within check-in flow and as standalone module from dashboard.

01
UPS-01 — Available Upgrades
S Room upgrade cards: current room vs upgrade. Photo comparison, amenity differences, price delta. "Upgrade for +$XX/night" CTA per option.
02
UPS-02 — Add-on Services

Ads System

3 ad types. Hotel configures per screen via CMS. Uploads .jpg assets.

01
ADS-01 — Banner Ad
O Horizontal strip. Positioned inline within a screen (between sections). Non-intrusive. Tappable → deeper info or external QR.
02
ADS-02 — Header Ad
O Top of screen bar. Slim. Hotel promo, event highlight, or sponsor. Auto-rotates if multiple configured.
03
ADS-03 — Popup Ad
M Modal overlay. Appears between flows (hotel configures which screen triggers it). "X" close button. Dismissible. Shows promotional image — hotel event, local business partner, seasonal offer.
Popup ads should NEVER appear during critical flows (payment, ID scan, key dispensing). Only on dashboard or between module transitions.

TruePay (Payment)

Shared payment flow used across all modules.

01
PAY-01 — Payment Screen
S Amount summary. "Please use the payment terminal on your right." Animation/arrow pointing to TruePay hardware. Accepted methods: tap, insert, swipe. HW Terminal activates.
02
PAY-02 — Processing
O "Processing payment..." Animated loader. Do not touch/remove card message.
03
PAY-03 — Success
O "Payment successful!" Check animation. Amount confirmed. Auto-advances to next step in flow.
Branch: Payment failed
03e
PAY-03e — Payment Failed
E "Payment declined. Please try another card." Retry button. After 3 attempts: "Request Assistance" button. Option to charge to room (if identified guest).

AI Avatar

HeyGen Interactive Avatar. Voice + video. Knows all hotel data. Chat bubble on dashboard, full-screen on open.

01
AVT-01 — Chat Bubble
O Floating bubble on dashboard bottom-right. Avatar thumbnail + "Need help?" label. Subtle idle animation (breathing, blinking). Tap to expand.
02
AVT-02 — Avatar Conversation
S Large video feed of AI avatar (HeyGen streaming). Avatar speaks and listens. Guest talks via microphone. Conversation transcript shown as chat bubbles alongside video. Quick action buttons: "Check-in help", "Find my room", "Room service", "Wi-Fi". "Close" button to minimize back to bubble. HW Mic + Speaker active.
Avatar knowledge base loaded with: hotel FAQ, amenities, hours, room types, local recommendations, check-in/out policies, event schedule. Updated via CMS sync.

23

Error States

E
ERR-01 — ID Scan Failed (retry)
"We couldn't read your document. Please reposition and try again." Attempt counter. After 3: help button.
E
ERR-02 — Facial Match Failed
"We couldn't verify your identity. Please try again." Lighting/position tips. After 3: help button.
E
ERR-03 — Payment Declined
"Payment was declined. Try another card or request assistance."
E
ERR-04 — Reservation Not Found
"We couldn't find a reservation with that information. Please check and try again." Option to try different method or request help.
E
ERR-05 — Key Dispenser Error
"There was an issue dispensing your key card. Please wait..." Auto-retry. If persists: "Please see the front desk for your key card. Your check-in is complete."
E
ERR-06 — Network Error
"Connection issue. Please wait a moment..." Auto-retry with countdown. If persists: "Please see the front desk for assistance."
E
ERR-07 — No Availability (Booking)
"No rooms available for your selected dates. Please try different dates or see the front desk."
E
ERR-08 — QR Code Invalid
"We couldn't read this QR code. Please try again or enter your reservation code manually."
E
ERR-09 — Payment Terminal Offline
"Payment terminal is temporarily unavailable. Please see the front desk." Or "Charge to room" option if guest is identified.
E
ERR-10 — Camera Error
"Camera is temporarily unavailable." Skip facial verification and proceed (with staff notification). Or request assistance.
E
ERR-11 — Room Service Unavailable
"Room service is currently unavailable. Please try again later or contact the front desk."
E
ERR-12 — Generic Error
"Something went wrong. Please try again." Retry + Request Assistance buttons. Fallback for uncategorized errors.
24

Inactivity Timer

01
INA-01 — Warning Modal
M Triggers at 45 seconds of inactivity. "Are you still there?" Circular countdown timer (15 seconds). Two buttons: "End Session" + "I'm Still Here". If timer hits 0 → session ends, returns to idle screensaver. All guest data cleared.
25

Staff Override

01
STF-01 — Secret Access
O 5 rapid taps on NEXI logo (anywhere it appears). Opens PIN entry. Or triggered by physical button on rear of kiosk.
02
STF-02 — PIN Entry
S 6-digit PIN keypad. 3 failed attempts → lockout for 5 minutes.
03
STF-03 — Admin Panel
S Simple dashboard: Restart session, Hardware status (green/red indicators for scanner, camera, dispenser, terminal, network), Maintenance mode toggle, Recent error log, Software version, "Exit Admin" button.
26

Receipt / Confirmation Delivery

Shared component used after check-in, check-out, booking, room service, payment.

01
RCT-01 — Delivery Options
S Three options: "Send to my email" (enter/confirm email) | "Send to my phone" (enter/confirm phone number) | "Show QR code" (scan to download). Guest picks one. "Skip" option available.
